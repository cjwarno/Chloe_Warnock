About My Photography

Why Photography

Creativity. To become a photographer gave me an entirely new perspective on everyday life. You can use photography to turn almost anything you find beautiful into a work of art. You can use your photographic skills to creatively capture an array of subjects that ranges from emotions to gatherings.

Influenced By

Sally Mann

An American photographer, widely known for her large-format, black-and-white photographs—at first of her young children, then later of landscapes suggesting decay and death.

Eugène Atget

Considered a pioneer of documentary photography, noted for his determination to document all of the architecture and street scenes of Paris before their disappearance to modernization. Most of his photographs were first published by Berenice Abbott after his death.

Garry Winogrand

An American street photographer, known for his portrayal of U.S. life and its social issues, in the mid-20th century.

Types of Cameras I Use

Nikon D50

The Nikon D50 is my favorite camera that I use.

  • It is Compact, Lightweight and Easy to Use
  • Has a high performance 6.1 Effective Megapixel Nikon DX Format CCD Image Sensor
  • Has 7 Digital Vari-Programs

Nikon N65

The Nikon N65 is an inexpensive camera that gives pretty good quality photos, although I have only used it a handfull of times

  • It has auto focus with 5 focus points and manual Focus
  • Has a 6 segment 3D Matrix Metering
  • Center Weighted Metering automatically selected in manual exposure mode

Canon PowerShot S3IS

The Canon PowerShot S3 IS was the first camera I bought.

  • It is evaluative and has a center-weighted average
  • Has a spot metering frame that is fixed to the center or linked to the AF frame

Lens
